The grammar

[count][operator][motion] — most edit commands fit this pattern.

  • 5dd — delete 5 lines
  • d3w — delete 3 words
  • y$ — yank to end of line
  • c2j — change 2 lines downward

Operators: d delete, y yank, c change, > indent, < dedent, = reformat.
